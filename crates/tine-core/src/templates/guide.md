- ## Create one yourself
  - 1. Open one of the Guide pages above and follow its numbered steps.
  - 2. Use **Copy the guide into your graph** when you want an editable sandbox.
  - 3. Tine creates the real `tine-guide/` namespace, rewrites guide links to that copy, and leaves existing copied pages untouched.
  - 4. What you should see: normal Logseq pages you can edit, delete, or keep as a reference.
